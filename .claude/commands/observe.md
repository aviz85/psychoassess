# Behavioral Observation Command

Begin behavioral observation documentation session.

## Instructions

Use the behavioral-observation skill to:

1. **Present the observation framework** - Show the key domains to observe
2. **Guide systematic documentation** - Prompt for observations in each area
3. **Organize notes** - Structure observations professionally
4. **Flag validity considerations** - Note factors that may affect interpretation
5. **Connect to testing** - Link behaviors to performance patterns

## Observation Domains to Cover

### 1. Physical Presentation
- Appearance, grooming, motor activity

### 2. Attention & Concentration
- Sustained attention, distractibility, redirections needed

### 3. Approach to Tasks
- Engagement, problem-solving style, response to difficulty

### 4. Rapport & Social Behavior
- Eye contact, reciprocity, communication style

### 5. Emotional Presentation
- Affect, mood, frustration tolerance

### 6. Test-Taking Behaviors
- Strategies, self-monitoring, effort

## Interactive Mode

Guide me through documenting observations for this session. Ask me questions about what I observed and help organize my notes into a coherent behavioral observations section.

At the end, provide:
- Organized observation notes
- Validity statement draft
- Notable behaviors for interpretation
