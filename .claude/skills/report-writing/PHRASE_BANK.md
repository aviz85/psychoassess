# Professional Phrase Bank for Psychological Reports

## Score Description Phrases

### Above Average Performance
- "demonstrated a notable strength in..."
- "performed significantly above age expectations in..."
- "showed well-developed skills in..."
- "exhibited particular proficiency in..."
- "displayed a relative strength in..."

### Average Performance
- "performed within the average range..."
- "demonstrated age-appropriate skills in..."
- "showed typical development in..."
- "exhibited expected performance in..."
- "fell within normal limits for..."

### Below Average Performance
- "demonstrated relative difficulty with..."
- "showed emerging skills in..."
- "displayed a pattern of weakness in..."
- "exhibited challenges in the area of..."
- "performed below age expectations in..."

### Significant Weakness
- "demonstrated significant difficulty with..."
- "showed a marked weakness in..."
- "displayed notable challenges in..."
- "exhibited substantial difficulty with..."
- "represents an area of significant need..."

## Behavioral Observation Phrases

### Attention/Concentration
**Positive**:
- "maintained consistent focus throughout testing"
- "demonstrated sustained attention to tasks"
- "remained engaged even during challenging items"

**Concerns**:
- "required frequent redirection to tasks"
- "demonstrated variable attention, particularly during..."
- "showed decreased focus as demands increased"
- "displayed difficulty sustaining attention on less engaging tasks"

### Effort/Motivation
**Positive**:
- "put forth consistent effort throughout the evaluation"
- "demonstrated strong motivation to succeed"
- "showed persistence even when tasks were challenging"
- "appeared invested in performing well"

**Concerns**:
- "showed variable effort across tasks"
- "appeared to give up quickly when frustrated"
- "demonstrated decreased motivation over time"

### Approach to Tasks
- "approached tasks in a reflective manner"
- "used a trial-and-error approach"
- "demonstrated systematic problem-solving"
- "showed impulsive responding on some tasks"
- "worked carefully and methodically"
- "appeared hesitant when uncertain"

### Emotional Presentation
- "presented with appropriate affect"
- "appeared somewhat anxious during testing"
- "maintained positive mood throughout"
- "showed frustration tolerance challenges"
- "demonstrated appropriate range of emotions"

## Interpretation Phrases

### Introducing Patterns
- "The pattern of scores suggests..."
- "This profile is consistent with..."
- "These results indicate..."
- "The discrepancy between X and Y suggests..."
- "Taken together, these findings point to..."

### Explaining Implications
- "This pattern may impact academic performance by..."
- "In the classroom, this may manifest as..."
- "Functionally, this means..."
- "Students with this profile typically..."
- "This finding has implications for..."

### Addressing Discrepancies
- "The significant difference between X and Y suggests..."
- "This discrepancy occurs in approximately X% of the population"
- "While statistically significant, this difference..."
- "The gap between these abilities may explain..."

### Integrating Findings
- "Consistent with this pattern, behavioral observations showed..."
- "This finding is supported by..."
- "The convergence of cognitive testing, achievement measures, and behavioral observations suggests..."
- "This interpretation is strengthened by..."

## Diagnostic Impression Phrases

### Supporting a Diagnosis
- "meets criteria for..."
- "presentation is consistent with..."
- "demonstrates the characteristic pattern of..."
- "findings support a diagnosis of..."
- "profile is typical of individuals with..."

### Ruling Out
- "does not meet full criteria for..."
- "although X was considered, the absence of Y suggests..."
- "while some features of X are present, the pattern is better explained by..."
- "was ruled out based on..."

### Uncertainty
- "findings are suggestive of, but not conclusive for..."
- "additional evaluation may be helpful to clarify..."
- "monitoring for emerging symptoms of X is recommended"
- "this presentation warrants continued observation of..."

## Recommendation Phrases

### Accommodations
- "To address X, it is recommended that..."
- "Given the identified weakness in X, [Name] would benefit from..."
- "Extended time will allow..."
- "Preferential seating will support..."
- "Breaking tasks into smaller steps will help..."

### Interventions
- "Structured intervention targeting X is recommended"
- "Evidence-based programs for X include..."
- "[Name] would benefit from direct instruction in..."
- "A multi-sensory approach to X is recommended"
- "Systematic practice with X will strengthen..."

### Strengths-Based
- "Leveraging [Name]'s strength in X to support Y is recommended"
- "[Name]'s strong X skills can be used to compensate for..."
- "Building on [Name]'s ability to..."

### Follow-Up
- "Progress monitoring in X is recommended"
- "Re-evaluation in X months/years is suggested to..."
- "Consultation with X is recommended to..."
- "Referral for X is warranted to..."

## Connecting Findings to Recommendations

### Template Phrases
- "Given [finding], [recommendation]"
- "Based on [finding], it is recommended that..."
- "To address [finding], [recommendation]"
- "[Finding] suggests that [Name] would benefit from..."
- "Because [finding], [recommendation] will help..."

### Examples
- "Given the identified working memory weakness, it is recommended that teachers provide written instructions in addition to verbal directions."
- "Based on processing speed difficulties, extended time on tests (1.5x) is recommended to allow [Name] to demonstrate her true knowledge without the pressure of time constraints."
- "To address the relative weakness in reading fluency, systematic practice with repeated reading exercises is recommended."

## Report Opening/Closing Phrases

### Opening Paragraphs
- "[Name] is a [age]-year-old [grade] student who was referred for..."
- "This evaluation was requested by [source] to address concerns about..."
- "[Name] was seen for psychoeducational evaluation on [dates]..."

### Summary Openings
- "In summary, this evaluation indicates..."
- "Overall, [Name] presents as a [description] student who..."
- "The results of this comprehensive evaluation suggest..."
- "Taken together, these findings indicate..."

### Closing Statements
- "With appropriate support, [Name] has the potential to..."
- "Implementation of these recommendations should support..."
- "It is hoped that these findings will help [Name]..."
- "Please contact the examiner if you have questions about this report or need clarification regarding recommendations."
